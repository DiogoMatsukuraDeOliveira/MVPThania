7) Estilos de Sombras

shadow-sm
shadow-md
shadow-lg
surface (cards)
nav (abas ativas)
focus ring

• sm = itens pequenos · md = botões/menus · lg = modais/overlays.
• surface = cards padrão · nav = elevação da aba ativa · focus ring = inputs (acento).

8) Bordas e Formas

radius-xs (6px)
radius-sm (8px)
radius-md (10px)
radius-lg (12px)
radius-xl (50px)
radius-full (circular)
border-soft (1px)
border-strong (2px)
pill (radius-xl)

• radius-xl = abas/botões pill · radius-lg = cards/modais · radius-xs = inputs.
• Bordas: soft (1px) para listas/cards; strong (2px) para destaques.
